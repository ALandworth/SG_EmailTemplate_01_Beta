As a follow-up to our recent outreach, Study Groups provides executive peer groups for general contractors, helping owners and senior leaders understand how their financial and operational performance truly compares by benchmarking against non-competing peers nationwide.


Most GC owners consider a 4 percent net margin a solid year.


However, data from the top 25 percent of firms in our network shows they consistently deliver 8 percent or higher net margins.


The difference is rarely effort or experience.

It is visibility.


Most owners know their own numbers, but without confidential peer benchmarks, it is difficult to know whether performance is genuinely strong or if margin is quietly leaking.


One of the biggest blind spots we see is Work in Progress, or WIP.


WIP determines how accurately revenue, margin, and cash flow are recognized acrosstudys active jobs. When WIP discipline is weak, firms often appear profitable on the P and L while margin erosion builds underneath the surface.


At Study Groups, we place contractors into confidential executive peer groups of non-competing firms and provide proprietary benchmarking across key performance areas, including:


WIP schedules and backlog health

Return on Capital Employed

Safety EMR scores and insurance costs

Labor and overhead efficiency


Firms that participate in a Study Group for five years or more achieve 50 percent higher Return on Capital Employed than comparable firms outside our network.
